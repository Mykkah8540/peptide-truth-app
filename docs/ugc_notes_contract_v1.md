# UGC Notes – Contract v1

Purpose:
- Capture experiential, real-world context without medical or dosing guidance.

Allowed:
- Why someone explored it
- What they noticed qualitatively
- What surprised them
- What to watch out for (non-actionable)

Explicitly Disallowed:
- Dosing amounts
- Frequencies
- Schedules
- Protocols
- “I took X mg” style content

Moderation:
- All submissions are pending by default
- Approved notes are immutable
- Rejected notes are stored with reason

Scope v1:
- Peptide pages
- Blend pages
- Stack pages (read-only for now)
